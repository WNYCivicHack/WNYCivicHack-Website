About

AT&T, United Way and UB ACM partnered together to come up with the all new WNY Civic Hack to be held in d!g Buffalo this coming fall.

This October 10 - 11, come join us to learn something new, build an app or start a company in our very own Buffalo.
Teams of up to four people spend the weekend with us working on innovative software and/or hardware solutions to help Buffalo grow into a smart city!

These projects can incorporate API's or platforms of all kinds which shall help the community advance.

Whether you want to develop the next big app, learn something new or just want to have fun, join us for what promises to be an exciting weekend!

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about the event?
Here are a few common questions we are asked.


What is the Civic App Challenge?

Civic App Challenge is a marathon software product creation event which is going to be hosted in the heart of Buffalo. This event gives you the flexibilty to make any app and get sponsorship from our sponsors and make your application go LIVE!

How long is the WNY Civic Hack Challenge?

WNYCivicHack starts October 10, 2015 (Saturday) 9am and goes on till 10pm.

The event will resume on October 11, 2015 (Sunday) at 10am which would include app and project submission followed by the results! (The event is not overnight)

What about travel?

We will be providing transportation to students of University at Buffalo from UB North Campus (More details coming soon)!

How do I register for the event?

You can sign-up for the event on EventBrite. Don't forget to bring your ticket on the day of the event :)

Who can attend this event?

Anyone above the age of 18 can attend WNY Civic Hack!

What should I bring?

All you would need is a valid student ID, your laptop + charger.

Will there be food provided?

Yes, meals will be provided!
