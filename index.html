WNY Civic Hackathon Rules

  • Teams can be up to four members.
  • Participants must submit their applications based on one of the civic themes.
  • Unfortunately we won't be able to provide the participants with any hardware products. Participants are encouraged to bring their own in case they wish to incorporate the same in their hack.
  • There will be topic specific datasets available for use for the participants. Participants can choose to use these datasets or can use any other open source available datasets. If you wish to use data off any research you have previously done, please cite the source.
  • All teams retain full ownership of what they have created during the hackathon.
  • Be respectful. Be thoughtful. Be open. Be awesome and have fun!

Demos

  • All teams are required to submit their hack. At least one team member should be present in order to give a demo for their hack to be presented.
  • Demos of your hack include but are not restricted to: Live Demos, Presentations, Code.
  • Hack Submissions should be straightforward and easy to understand.
  • Mockups or anything already launched is strictly prohibited from submission. Any attempt of doing so can lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Time Limit:
    Each demo should be not more than 300 seconds (5 minutes). Presenters will be told ahead of time and will be told how much time remains during the presentation.

Civic App Challenge is a marathon software product creation event which is going to be hosted in the heart of Buffalo. This event gives you the flexibilty to make any app and get sponsorship from our sponsors and make your application go LIVE!

How long is the WNY Civic Hack Challenge?

You can sign-up for the event on EventBrite (registration is free). Entry is only permitted with a valid ticket, so don't forget to bring your ticket on the day of the event!

Sorry, we only issue one ticket per person. While you're most welcome to work in a team at the event, you team members will have to register individually

Anyone above the age of 18 can attend WNY Civic Hack!
